### **Task 1 Write-up**

For Task 1, I designed a "Digital Literacy Awareness" infographic to visually communicate essential digital skills to my batchmates. I chose Canva to create this resource due to its intuitive interface and professional design elements. My infographic breaks down digital awareness into four distinct, easy-to-read topics. Topic 1 defines digital literacy as the ability to use digital tools effectively and communicate safely online. Topic 2 outlines safe internet practices, emphasizing the use of strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links. Topic 3 focuses on proper email etiquette, highlighting the importance of clear subject lines and formal language. Finally, Topic 4 lists useful tools for students, specifically mentioning Google Workspace, online learning platforms, and GitHub. 

While creating this, the most challenging aspect was condensing broad concepts into brief, digestible bullet points without losing their core meaning. However, I found it incredibly interesting to experiment with color gradients and visual hierarchy to guide the reader's eye smoothly from one topic to the next. Designing this infographic reinforced that effectively presenting information is just as vital as the information itself.
